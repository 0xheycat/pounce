# Pounce — Positioning & Launch Hook

This is the north star for how we describe Pounce. Goal: introduce it as a **fresh, useful contribution** to the download-manager space — never by attacking paid or existing tools.

## One-liner

> **Pounce is a self-hostable download engine you fully own — with permanent resume, multi-connection speed, and a beautiful 3D dashboard you can open from any browser.**

## The core idea (the "new category" angle)

Most download tools are *apps you install on one computer*. Pounce reframes downloading as **a small service you host** — on your laptop, home server, Raspberry Pi, or NAS — and control from any device on your network.

That single shift unlocks the hook: **"your own personal download service, open source and entirely yours."**

## Why this is genuinely useful for everyone

- **Own your data & history** — nothing leaves your hardware. No accounts, no telemetry, no cloud.
- **Runs anywhere, controlled everywhere** — one engine, one web UI, every OS and screen.
- **Featherweight** — a single static Go binary, no Electron/JVM. Happy on a $35 Pi.
- **Hackable** — clean REST + SSE API and a tiny, readable codebase made to be forked.
- **Free, MIT, forever** — useful to students, homelabbers, archivists, researchers, and anyone on a flaky connection.

## Principles (so we never "step on" anyone)

1. **Acknowledge the giants.** aria2, yt-dlp, JDownloader, IDM and others paved this road. We say so, openly, in the README.
2. **Cooperate, don't compete.** Where it helps, Pounce *uses* great tools (e.g. yt-dlp for media resolution) instead of replacing them.
3. **Never disparage.** No "better than X", no "X is bloated/dead" in our copy. We describe what Pounce *is*, not what others aren't.
4. **No trademarks in our name/marketing.** We don't ride on other products' names for clout.
5. **Honest status.** Alpha is alpha. We don't overpromise.

## Words we use / avoid

- ✅ Use: self-hostable, own, resume, single binary, hackable, open, cooperate, lightweight, yours.
- 🚫 Avoid: "IDM killer", "better than", "replacement for", "X is dead/bloated", any paid product's name as a selling point.

## The hook, distilled

**Self-hosted. Single binary. Resumes anything. Runs on a Pi. Looks gorgeous. Yours.**
